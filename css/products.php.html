Products

Quality

PRODUCING QUALITY FOR THE WORLD

Company’s Iron Oxide Pellets conform to international standards, making them ideal for hi-tech steel plants anywhere in the world. As a result of latest production techniques and constant R&D efforts of Company to improve quality, Company’s products are currently the choice of quality conscious consumer in India and Abroad with a highly satisfied clientele built up over the years.

ETCHING INTERNATIONAL QUALITY RATING

Company’s total quality movement is an assurance for the very best that it can offer. Right from the beginning what sets Company apart has been its commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. It is this consciousness for quality that led to certification of Quality Management System as per ISO-9001:2004 Standard.

CAPTIVE BERTH

KIOCL has a captive berth with mechanized loading facilities. The berth details are as under 

Loading Part New Mangalore Port, India
Loading Facility Mechanical Loading
Sailing Draft 12. to 12.5 metres with 1 metre tide (depending upon the season).
LOa 245 metres max
beam 20 to 32.2 metres
Air Draft 13.75 metres Max

EXPORTS

 

KIOCL is an Export Oriented Unit ( EOU) and a Government of India Enterprise under the Ministry Of Steel engaged in manufacturing and Marketing of iron ore pellets.   KIOCL sells pellets in overseas markets among empanelled customers on FOB ST New Mangalore Port basis.

  

DOMESTIC (DTA)

KIOCL sells pellets in Domestic (DTA) markets through Sea and Road among empanelled customers.
